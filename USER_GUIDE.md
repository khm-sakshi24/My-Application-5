# College Management App - User Guide

Welcome to the College Management App! This guide will walk you through the main features and how to use them.

## 1. Getting Started: First-Time Users

### Creating an Account

1.  When you first open the app, you will see the login screen.
2.  Tap the **"Need an account? Sign Up"** link at the bottom.
3.  Enter your email address and choose a secure password (must be at least 6 characters).
4.  Tap the **"Sign Up"** button.

Upon successful registration, you will be automatically logged in and taken to your dashboard. By default, all new users are assigned the **Student** role.

## 2. Logging In and Out

*   **To Log In**: On the main screen, enter the email and password you used to register and tap the **"Login"** button.
*   **To Log Out**: From your main dashboard, tap the **"Sign Out"** button at the bottom of the screen.

## 3. Using the App as a Student

As a student, your dashboard provides access to your academic information.

*   **Dashboard**: Your main screen will greet you and display your role as "Student".
*   **View Grades**: You will see a primary action card labeled **"View My Grades"**. Tapping this will take you to a screen where you can view your current grades and academic progress (Note: This screen is a placeholder for future development).

## 4. Using the App as a Teacher

To access teacher functionalities, your account must be assigned the **Teacher** role by an administrator in the Firebase console.

*   **Dashboard**: Your main screen will greet you and display your role as "Teacher".
*   **Manage Grades**: You will see a primary action card labeled **"Manage Student Grades"**. Tapping this will take you to a screen where you can view student rosters and update their grades (Note: This screen is a placeholder for future development).

## 5. Push Notifications

The app uses push notifications to send important alerts, such as grade updates or campus announcements. When a notification arrives, it will appear in your device's notification tray. Simply tap it to open the app.
